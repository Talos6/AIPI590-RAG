**Lisa**

**Title:** Rose Witch  
**Full Name:** Lisa Minci  
**Nation:** Mondstadt  
**Origin:** Unknown  
**Race:** Human  
**Gender:** Female  
**Rarity:** 4-Star  
**Standard/Limited:** Standard  
**Vision:** Electro  
**Weapon Type:** Catalyst  
**Constellation:** Tempus Fugit  
**Special Dish:** Mysterious Bolognese  
**Release Date:** September 15, 2020 (Version 1.0)  
**Tags:** Defense Reduction, Alchemy Refund, Interruption Resistance, Energy Recharge  

**Description:**
A lazy yet highly knowledgeable librarian, Lisa is a prodigy graduate from the Sumeru Akademiya, noted as a "once-in-two-centuries" genius.

**Recommended Builds**

### **Support + Sub-DPS**
- **Artifact Set:**
  - Universal: 4-piece Noblesse Oblige or 4-piece Emblem of Severed Fate
  - Optional: 4-piece Thundering Fury, 4-piece Thunderclap Vigor, or 4-piece Gilded Dreams
  - Dendro-Reaction Support: 4-piece Deepwood Memories
  - Transitional: 2-piece Thundering Fury + 2-piece Gladiator’s Finale or 2-piece Noblesse Oblige + any other piece
- **Main Stats:**
  - Sands: Energy Recharge, ATK%
  - Goblet: Electro DMG Bonus
  - Circlet: Crit DMG, Crit Rate
- **Substats:** Crit Rate, Crit DMG > Energy Recharge > ATK% > Elemental Mastery (for Aggravate)
- **Recommended Weapons:**
  - Best: 4-star Hakushin Ring, 3-star Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers
  - Alternatives: 5-star Kagura’s Verity, 4-star The Widsith, 5-star A Thousand Floating Dreams

### **Hyperbloom Reaction Build**
- **Artifact Set:**
  - Best: 4-piece Flower of Paradise Lost
  - Optional: 4-piece Thundering Fury or 4-piece Gilded Dreams
  - Mixed: 2-piece Wanderer’s Troupe + 2-piece Gilded Dreams
- **Main Stats:**
  - Sands: Elemental Mastery
  - Goblet: Elemental Mastery

- **Substats:** Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge
- **Recommended Weapons:**
  - Best: 5-star A Thousand Floating Dreams
  - Alternatives: 4-star Sacrificial Fragments, 4-star Wandering Evenstar

**Team Compositions**

### **Aggravate Lisa Team**
- **Members:** Lisa, Nahida, Fischl, Kazuha
- **Description:**
  - Lisa serves as an Electro enabler and defense shredder.
  - Nahida provides consistent Dendro application for Aggravate reactions.
  - Fischl offers additional off-field Electro damage and energy generation.
  - Kazuha provides crowd control and elemental resistance reduction.

### **Hyperbloom Lisa Team**
- **Members:** Lisa, Nahida, Xingqiu, Kuki Shinobu
- **Description:**
  - Lisa’s Elemental Skill can trigger Hyperbloom with Electro attacks.
  - Nahida provides consistent Dendro application.
  - Xingqiu provides Hydro application for Bloom reactions.
  - Kuki Shinobu serves as both a healer and an additional Electro trigger.
