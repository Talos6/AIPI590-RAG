## **Recommended Builds**

### **Artifact Sets**

#### **Elemental Reaction Build**  
- **4-Piece Crimson Witch of Flames**
- **4-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence**
- **4-Piece Marechaussee Hunter + any off-piece**  
- **Mixed:** 2-Piece Crimson Witch of Flames + 2-Piece Gladiator’s Finale or another 2-Piece ATK% bonus artifact  

**Recommended Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK%  
- **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus  
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG  
- **Substats Priority:** CRIT Rate ≥ CRIT DMG > ATK% > Flat ATK  

#### **Pure Pyro Build**  
- **4-Piece Lavawalker**
- **4-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence**
- **4-Piece Marechaussee Hunter + any off-piece**

### **Mono Pyro Team**

- **Bennett** (ATK Buff & Healing)  
- **Xiangling** (Off-Field Pyro DMG)  
- **Kazuha / Zhongli** (Crowd Control / Shielding)  

### **Reverse Vaporize Team**  
- **Klee** (Main DPS)  
- **Furina** (HP Burn & Hydro Application)  
- **Kazuha / Sucrose** (Crowd Control & Elemental DMG Boost)  
- **Bennett / Sigewinne** (Healing & ATK Buff)  

---

Klee is a powerful Pyro DPS with explosive abilities, excellent AoE coverage, and high Pyro application. However, her stamina consumption and squishiness require strategic positioning and team synergy. Properly built and supported, she can be a formidable force in battle!
