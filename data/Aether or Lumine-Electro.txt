**Description:** A traveler drifting from beyond this world. After their sibling was taken by a god, they embarked on a journey to seek the Seven Archons.

---

**Constellation Effects:**

1. **Thundering Springtime** – Increases the number of Abundance Amulets created by Lightning Blade to 3.
2. **Furious Thunderstorm** – After Lightning Blast hits an opponent, their Electro Resistance is reduced by 15% for 8 seconds.
3. **Distant Rolling Thunder** – Increases the level of Lightning Blast by 3, up to a maximum of level 15.
4. **Unpredictable Thunderclouds** – When a character obtains an Abundance Amulet generated by Lightning Blade, if their Energy is below 35%, the Energy restored is increased by 100%.
5. **Wild Thunder in the Wastes** – Increases the level of Lightning Blade by 3, up to a maximum of level 15.
6. **World-Shaking Thunder** – Every 2 instances of Falling Thunder from Lightning Blast increase the damage of the next Falling Thunder by 100% and restore 1 Energy to the current character.

### **Lightning Blade**

**Description:**
Performs three quick Electro slashes, leaving behind Abundance Amulets upon hitting opponents. Initially generates up to two Abundance Amulets, which are cleared upon skill activation.

**Abundance Amulet:**
- Restores Energy when collected.
- Increases Energy Recharge for the duration.

| Level | Skill DMG | Energy Restored | Energy Recharge Boost | Duration | Amulet Duration | Cooldown |
|-------|----------|----------------|-----------------------|----------|----------------|----------|
| Lv. 1 | 78.7%   | 3.0             | 20%                   | 6s       | 15s            | 13.5s    |
| Lv. 10 | 141.6%  | 4.0             | 20%                   | 6s       | 15s            | 13.5s    |

---

### **Lightning Blast**

**Description:**
Summons the protection of thunder, dealing Electro DMG and knocking back surrounding enemies.

**Thunderous Protection:**
- Normal and Charged Attacks trigger Falling Thunder, dealing Electro DMG.
- Falling Thunder restores Energy.
- Falling Thunder can be triggered once every 0.5s.

| Level | Skill DMG | Falling Thunder DMG | Energy Restored | Duration | Cooldown | Energy Cost |
|-------|----------|--------------------|----------------|----------|----------|------------|
| Lv. 1 | 114.4%  | 32.8%              | 0.8            | 12s      | 20s      | 80         |
| Lv. 10 | 205.9%  | 59.0%              | 1.0            | 12s      | 20s      | 80         |

---

### **Swift Thunder's Resurgence**

**Description:**
When a nearby party member collects an Abundance Amulet from Lightning Blade, Lightning Blade's cooldown is reduced by 1.5s.

---

### **Resounding Thunder**

**Description:**
Increases the Energy Recharge effect of Abundance Amulets based on 10% of the Traveler’s Energy Recharge.
