### **Summary of Playstyle**
- Jiaming specializes in high-damage **Plunging Attacks** empowered by his Elemental Skill.
- His Elemental Burst resets the cooldown of his Skill, allowing for **continuous damage rotations**.
- He has **self-healing and interruption resistance**, making him durable in combat.
- He benefits most from **Pyro Elemental Reactions**, such as Vaporize or Melt.

Jiaming’s ideal role is as a **Plunging Attack DPS** who cycles through Elemental Skill and Burst to maintain **high Pyro uptime** and **consistent damage output**.
