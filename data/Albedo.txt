**Origin:** Khaenri'ah  
**Race:** Alchemical Creation  
**Gender:** Male  
**Rarity:** 5-star  
**Availability:** Limited UP  
**Vision:** Geo

**Primordial Power:**

**Tags:** Damage Boost, Elemental Mastery Bonus, Alchemy Refund, Coordinated Attack, Self-Damage Boost, Geo Constructs  

**Introduction:**  
The Chief Alchemist of the Knights of Favonius and Captain of the Investigation Team, Albedo is a genius often referred to as the "Chalk Prince."

**Recommended Builds**

**Playstyles:**
- Off-field DPS
- General Support

**Best Artifact Sets:**  
- **Best-in-Slot:** 4-Piece Husk of Opulent Dreams or 4-Piece Golden Troupe  
- **Transitional:** 4-Piece Echoes of an Offering + Any piece  
- **Mixed:** 2-Piece Husk of Opulent Dreams + 2-Piece Golden Troupe or 2-Piece Archaic Petra + Any piece  

**Recommended Artifact Stats:**
- **Sands:** DEF%  
- **Goblet:** Geo DMG Bonus (Husk set), DEF% (Golden Troupe set)  
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG  

**Substat Priorities:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG > DEF% > Flat DEF  

**Weapons:**
- **Best 5-Star:** Cinnabar Spindle (4-star), Haran Geppaku Futsu (5-star)  
- **Alternative 4-Star:** Serpent Spine, Wolf-Fang  
- **Alternative 3-Star:** Harbinger of Dawn  
- **Other Viable Options:** Primordial Jade Cutter, Freedom-Sworn  

**Skill Leveling Priority:**
1. Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst  
2. Do not level Normal Attack
