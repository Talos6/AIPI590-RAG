### **Build Recommendations**

#### **Recommended Artifact Sets**

- **General Build (Off-Field DPS):** 4-piece **Golden Troupe** + any piece  
- **Hyperbloom Main DPS:** 4-piece **Gilded Dreams** + any piece  
- **Support Buffing:** 4-piece **Tenacity of the Millelith**  
- **Bloom-Reaction Build:** 4-piece **Paradise Lost**  

#### **Main Stats:**
- **Sands:** ATK% / Elemental Mastery  
- **Goblet:** Electro DMG Bonus / ATK% (for Golden Troupe)  
- **Circlet:** Crit Rate / Crit DMG  

#### **Sub-Stats Priority:**
**Crit Rate / Crit DMG > ATK% > Elemental Mastery**  

---

### **Talent Leveling Priority**
**Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst > Normal Attack**  
- **Elemental Skill (Sesshou Sakura):** Yae Miko’s **primary source of damage**, making it the highest priority.  
- **Elemental Burst (Tenko Kenshin):** Deals **high burst damage**, but has a **high energy cost** (90).  
- **Normal Attack:** Not recommended for leveling unless playing a rare Main DPS build.  

---

### **Weapon Recommendations**

#### **Best 5-Star Weapons:**
1. **Kagura’s Verity (Best-in-Slot)**  
   - **High Crit DMG and Skill DMG Buffs** make it Yae Miko’s best weapon.  
2. **Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds**  
   - **High Crit Rate and Movement Speed Buffs** but loses effect when switching.  
3. **Skyward Atlas**  
   - **High Base ATK and ATK% Buffs**, good for ATK-scaling builds.  
4. **Tulaytullah’s Remembrance**  
   - **Main DPS weapon**, boosts **Normal and Charged Attack DMG**, niche for Aggravate playstyles.  

#### **Best 4-Star Weapons:**
1. **The Widsith**  
   - **High Crit DMG** with strong **random buffs**.  
2. **Solar Pearl**  
   - **Crit Rate weapon** (Battle Pass), consistent damage boost.  
3. **Flowing Purity**  
   - **Elemental Mastery-based weapon**, great for Hyperbloom.  
4. **Hakushin Ring**  
   - **Good for team synergy**, boosts **Electro DMG Bonus** after reaction triggers.

---

### **Final Notes**
Yae Miko is a **strong off-field Electro DPS** with **high scaling**, making her a **top-tier Sub-DPS** in Aggravate and Hyperbloom teams. With **proper investment in Crit stats and Elemental Mastery**, she can significantly boost team DPS. However, her **high Elemental Burst energy cost** means **battery supports** like **Raiden Shogun or Fischl** are highly recommended.
