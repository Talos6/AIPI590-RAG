**Origin:** Unknown  
**Race:** Human  
**Gender:** Female  
**Rarity:** 4-Star  
**Standard/Limited:** Standard  
**Vision:** Electro

**Constellation:** Tribulatio Demptio

**Release Date:** June 21, 2022 (Version 2.7)  
**Tags:** Healing, Expedition Bonus, HP Drain, Skill Extension, Self-Damage Boost

**Team Compositions**

### **Hyperbloom Team**
- **Members:** Kuki Shinobu, Nahida, Xingqiu, Fischl
- **Description:**
  - Kuki Shinobu applies Electro for triggering Hyperbloom.
  - Nahida provides consistent Dendro application.
  - Xingqiu applies Hydro for Bloom reactions.
  - Fischl provides additional off-field Electro damage.

### **Aggravate Team**
- **Members:** Kuki Shinobu, Nahida, Yae Miko, Kazuha
- **Description:**
  - Shinobu acts as both a healer and an Aggravate enabler.
  - Nahida provides Dendro application.
  - Yae Miko adds off-field Electro damage.
  - Kazuha provides crowd control and resistance reduction.
