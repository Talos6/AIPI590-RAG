### **Skill Leveling Priority**
- **Main Build (Bloom Support)**: **No need to level up any skills** (since Bloom reaction scales with Level and Elemental Mastery, not talent levels).
- **Alternate Build (Hydro DPS)**: **Elemental Skill** = **Elemental Burst** > **Normal Attack**

**Explanation**:
- Nilou’s Bloom reaction damage is purely based on Level and Elemental Mastery, making talent levels irrelevant.
- If playing as an on-field Hydro DPS, leveling **Elemental Skill** and **Burst** will maximize damage output.
