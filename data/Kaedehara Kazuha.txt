**Kaedehara Kazuha - Character Information**

**Title:** Scarlet Leaves Pursue Wild Waves  
**Full Name:** Kaedehara Kazuha  
**Nation:** Inazuma  
**Origin:** Unknown  
**Race:** Human  
**Gender:** Male  
**Rarity:** ★★★★★  
**Availability:** Limited UP  
**Vision:** Anemo  
**Weapon:** Sword  
**Archon Power:** N/A  
**Constellation:** Acer Palmatum  
**Special Dish:** "All-Weather Beauty"  
**Release Date:** June 29, 2021 (Version 1.6)  
**Tags:** Damage Boost, Crowd Control, Sprint Stamina Reduction, Elemental Mastery Buff, Cooldown Reduction, Energy Recharge, Self Damage Boost, Elemental Infusion  

**Introduction:**  
A wandering samurai from Inazuma, now residing among the "Crux Fleet" in Liyue. He has a calm and free-spirited nature but harbors many past stories within his heart.

### **Recommended Builds**

#### **Support Build (Damage Boosting)**

- **Artifact Set:** 4-Piece Viridescent Venerer
- **Main Stats:**
  - Sands: Elemental Mastery
  - Goblet: Elemental Mastery
  - Circlet: Elemental Mastery
- **Substats:** Elemental Mastery > Crit Rate (if using Favonius Sword) > Energy Recharge > Crit Rate, Crit DMG
- **Weapons:**
  - **5★ Freedom-Sworn:** Best-in-slot weapon for elemental mastery and team buffs.
  - **4★ Xiphos’ Moonlight:** Provides Elemental Mastery and Energy Recharge boost for the team.
  - **4★ Favonius Sword:** Helps energy regeneration, ensuring frequent bursts.
  - **4★ Sacrificial Sword:** Allows skill cooldown resets for additional Swirl reactions.
  - **3★ Dark Iron Sword:** Good free-to-play option with Elemental Mastery.

#### **DPS Build (Pyro or Anemo Main DPS)**

- **Artifact Set:**
  - **Fire-infused:** 4-Piece Crimson Witch of Flames or 4-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence (if using Bennett C6)
  - **Anemo-infused:** 4-Piece Desert Pavilion Chronicle or 2-Piece VV + 2-Piece Attack %
- **Main Stats:**
  - Sands: Elemental Mastery / ATK%
  - Goblet: Pyro DMG Bonus / Anemo DMG Bonus
  - Circlet: Crit Rate / Crit DMG
- **Substats:** Crit Rate, Crit DMG, Elemental Mastery, ATK%
- **Weapons:**
  - **5★ Primordial Jade Cutter:** Provides Crit Rate and bonus ATK scaling on HP.
  - **5★ Mistsplitter Reforged:** Works well for Elemental-infused Kazuha.
  - **5★ Freedom-Sworn:** Ideal for EM scaling Pyro-infused Kazuha.
  - **4★ The Alley Flash:** Provides high base ATK and Elemental Mastery.
  - **4★ Blackcliff Longsword:** Offers Crit DMG but requires stacking kills.
  - **3★ Harbinger of Dawn:** Great free-to-play option if maintaining high HP.

### **Team Compositions**

#### **Elemental Support (Swirl Boosting)**
- **Kazuha + Pyro/Cryo/Electro/Hydro DPS + Bennett/Kokomi + Xiangling/Fischl/Yelan**
- Focuses on Kazuha’s Swirl debuff and elemental amplification.

#### **Pyro Infusion Main DPS (C6 Required)**
- **Kazuha (Pyro-infused) + Bennett C6 + Xiangling + Xingqiu/Yelan**
- Relies on Pyro infusion for melee combat and Vaporize reactions.

#### **Electro-Charged Support**
- **Kazuha + Raiden Shogun + Fischl + Kokomi**
- Generates Electro-Charged reactions and buffs team Elemental DMG.

#### **Mono-Anemo Team**
- **Kazuha + Xiao + Jean + Faruzan**
- Buffs Anemo DMG and provides swirl resistance shred.
