**Team Compositions**

### **Electro Hypercarry**
- **Members:** Kujou Sara, Raiden Shogun, Kazuha, Bennett
- **Description:**
  - Kujou Sara provides ATK and Electro Crit DMG boost.
  - Raiden Shogun acts as the main Electro DPS.
  - Kazuha provides crowd control and Electro resistance shred.
  - Bennett boosts ATK and heals the team.

### **Aggravate Team**
- **Members:** Kujou Sara, Nahida, Yae Miko, Kazuha
- **Description:**
  - Kujou Sara provides Electro buffing.
  - Nahida ensures consistent Dendro application for Aggravate reactions.
  - Yae Miko acts as a strong off-field Electro DPS.
  - Kazuha provides crowd control and resistance shred.
