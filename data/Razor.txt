---

**Playstyle Recommendations**
- Razor thrives as a Physical DPS with sustained Normal Attacks during his Elemental Burst. 
- Due to his fast attack speed and self-buffing abilities, he pairs well with teams that can support his damage output without interrupting his attack flow. 
- Elemental Mastery builds can be effective in Electro-based reaction teams, particularly with Dendro units for Aggravate reactions. 
- Shield supports like Zhongli or Diona can help maintain his buffs and increase survivability.
