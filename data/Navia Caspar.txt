## **Constellation**

| Name | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Rules of a Lady’s Distance** | When **Ceremonial Crystalshot** is cast, each "Crystal Shard" consumed restores **3 Energy** to Navia and reduces the cooldown of **As the Sunlit Sky’s Ceremonial Salute** by 1s. A maximum of **9 Energy** can be restored, and the cooldown can be reduced by up to 3s. |
| **Commander’s Relentless Pursuit** | When **Ceremonial Crystalshot** is cast, each "Crystal Shard" consumed increases its CRIT Rate by **12%**, up to a maximum of **36%**. Additionally, when **Ceremonial Crystalshot** hits an enemy, it summons an extra **Support Bombardment** from **As the Sunlit Sky’s Ceremonial Salute** at the enemy’s location. This effect can trigger once per cast, and the bombardment counts as **Elemental Burst DMG**. |
| **Entrepreneur’s Expansive Outlook** | Increases the level of **Ceremonial Crystalshot** by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **Oathbound’s Unyielding Judgment** | When **As the Sunlit Sky’s Ceremonial Salute** hits an enemy, it reduces their **Geo RES by 20%** for 8 seconds. |
| **Negotiator’s Firm Stance** | Increases the level of **As the Sunlit Sky’s Ceremonial Salute** by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **Spina di Rosula’s Adaptability** | When **Ceremonial Crystalshot** is cast and consumes more than 3 "Crystal Shards," each additional shard increases the **CRIT DMG of the shot by 45%** and the excess shards are refunded after casting. |

---

Navia is a powerful **Geo DPS** who scales with ATK rather than DEF, making her unique among Geo characters. Her **Crystallized Shards** are essential for maximizing her **Elemental Skill damage**, and she pairs well with **Geo resonance teams** or **Pyro characters for reaction-based damage**. With the right build and team, she can deal **high burst damage** while maintaining **consistent on-field presence**.
