**Kaeya**

**Title**: Frostwind Swordsman  
**Full Name**: Kaeya Alberich  
**Nation**: Mondstadt  
**Birthplace**: Khaenri’ah  
**Race**: Human  
**Gender**: Male  
**Rarity**: 4-Star  
**Availability**: Permanent  
**Vision**: Cryo  
**Weapon**: Sword  
**Constellation**: Pavo Ocellus  
**Special Dish**: Fruit-Glazed Roast  
**Release Date**: September 15, 2020 (Version 1.0)  
**Tags**: Shield, Energy Regeneration, Sprint Stamina Reduction, Self-Healing, Skill Duration Extension, Energy Recovery, Self-Damage Buff  

**Introduction**: A swordsman with an exotic appearance, Kaeya is known as the strategist of the Knights of Favonius.

---

### **Constellation**

| Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Excellent Bloodline** | Increases CRIT Rate of Normal and Charged Attacks by 15% against enemies affected by Cryo. |
| **Unyielding Frost** | When defeating an enemy during Glacial Waltz's duration, the duration is extended by 2.5 seconds, up to a maximum of 15 seconds. |
| **Frostbite Waltz** | Increases the level of Frostgnaw by 3, up to a maximum of 15. |
| **Cold-Blooded Strike** | When Kaeya's HP falls below 20%, a shield is automatically generated, absorbing damage equal to 30% of max HP for 20 seconds. This shield has a 250% absorption efficiency against Cryo damage. Can only occur once every 60 seconds. |
| **Frozen Embrace** | Increases the level of Glacial Waltz by 3, up to a maximum of 15. |
| **Glacial Whirlwind** | Glacial Waltz summons an additional icicle and restores 15 Energy upon activation. |

### **Recommended Builds**

#### **Sub-DPS Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Emblem of Severed Fate*
  - 4-piece *Blizzard Strayer* (for Freeze comps)
  - 2-piece *Blizzard Strayer* + 2-piece *Noblesse Oblige*

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: ATK% / Energy Recharge
  - **Goblet**: Cryo DMG Bonus
  - **Circlet**: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG

- **Substats**:
  - Energy Recharge, CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%

- **Skill Leveling Priority**:
  - Elemental Burst > Elemental Skill (Normal Attack not needed)

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *Primordial Jade Cutter* (5★)
  - *Mistsplitter Reforged* (5★)

- **Alternative Weapons**:
  - *Skyward Blade* (5★)
  - *Favonius Sword* (4★)
  - *Sacrificial Sword* (4★)
  - *Amenoma Kageuchi* (4★)

#### **Cryo Main DPS Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Blizzard Strayer*
  - 2-piece *Blizzard Strayer* + 2-piece *Wanderer’s Troupe*

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: ATK%
  - **Goblet**: Cryo DMG Bonus
  - **Circlet**: CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG

- **Substats**:
  - CRIT DMG > CRIT Rate, ATK% > Elemental Mastery

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *Mistsplitter Reforged* (5★)
  - *Primordial Jade Cutter* (5★)

- **Alternative Weapons**:
  - *Aquila Favonia* (5★)
  - *Summit Shaper* (5★)
  - *Primordial Jade Cutter* (5★)

#### **Physical Main DPS Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Gladiator’s Finale*
  - 4-piece *Pale Flame*
  - 2-piece *Pale Flame* + 2-piece *Bloodstained Chivalry*

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: ATK%
  - **Goblet**: Physical DMG Bonus
  - **Circlet**: CRIT Rate

- **Substats**:
  - CRIT DMG, CRIT Rate, ATK%

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *Aquila Favonia* (5★)

- **Alternative Weapons**:
  - *The Alley Flash* (4★)
  - *Prototype Rancour* (4★)
  - *The Flute* (4★)
  - *Primordial Jade Cutter* (5★)
  - *Summit Shaper* (5★)

Kaeya is a versatile Cryo character who excels in Freeze, Melt, and Physical damage builds. He offers strong burst damage, energy regeneration, and survivability, making him a valuable asset in many teams.
