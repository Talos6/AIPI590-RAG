## **Recommended Builds**

### **Artifact Sets**

#### **Main DPS (On-Field Carry)**  
- **4-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence**
- **4-Piece Echoes of an Offering**
- **4-Piece Blizzard Strayer** + any off-piece  

**Recommended Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK%  
- **Goblet:** Cryo DMG Bonus  
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG  
- **Substats Priority:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Elemental Mastery (if playing Melt), Energy Recharge  

#### **Sub DPS (Quick Swap Burst Damage)**  
- **4-Piece Blizzard Strayer**
- **4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate**
- **4-Piece Noblesse Oblige**
- **2-Piece Blizzard Strayer + 2-Piece Noblesse Oblige**  

**Recommended Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK% / Energy Recharge  
- **Goblet:** Cryo DMG Bonus  
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG  
- **Substats Priority:** CRIT DMG, CRIT Rate, ATK%, Energy Recharge  

---

### **Recommended Weapons**

#### **Best 5★ Weapons**  
- **Thundering Pulse** – High CRIT DMG, excellent for normal attack-focused builds.
- **Skyward Harp** – High base ATK and increased CRIT Rate & DMG.
- **Amos’ Bow** – Boosts Normal and Charged Attack DMG, works well with Cryo infusion.
- **Polar Star** – Requires skillful play to maintain stacks but provides strong ATK and CRIT boosts.

#### **Recommended 4★ Weapons**  
- **Predator** – Aloy’s signature weapon (only fully functional on PS versions).
- **Rust** – Excellent for normal attack-focused playstyles.
- **Favonius Warbow** – If Energy Recharge is a concern.
- **Mitternachts Waltz** – Provides a strong balance of Normal and Elemental Skill DMG.
- **Prototype Crescent** – Boosts Charged Attack DMG when hitting weak points.

#### **Weapons to Avoid**  
- **Elegy for the End** – More suited for a full support role rather than DPS.

## **Team Compositions**

### **Cryo Melt Team**  
- **Aloy** (Main DPS)  
- **Bennett** (ATK Buff & Healing)  
- **Xiangling** (Off-Field Pyro Application)  
- **Kazuha / Sucrose** (Crowd Control & Elemental DMG Boost)  

### **Freeze Team**  
- **Aloy** (Main DPS)  
- **Xingqiu / Mona** (Hydro Application)  
- **Rosaria / Kaeya** (Cryo Battery & Resonance)  
- **Diona** (Healing & Shielding)  

### **Quick Swap Burst Team**  
- **Aloy** (Sub-DPS)  
- **Ayaka / Ganyu** (Main DPS)  
- **Venti / Kazuha** (Crowd Control)  
- **Diona / Kokomi** (Healing & Support)  

---

Aloy is a unique crossover character with a versatile playstyle, excelling in Cryo-based reactions such as Melt and Freeze. While she lacks constellations, she can still be a strong pick when properly built and paired with the right teammates. Her Elemental Burst is quick and low-cost, making her a solid Sub-DPS or burst character in a Freeze or Melt team.
