**Constellation Effects**  

| Constellation Name | Effect |
|-------------------|--------|
| **Fortress of Dreams** | The damage absorption of the Curtain of Slumber shield generated by Nights of Formal Focus is increased by 20%. Additionally, when using Nights of Formal Focus, all nearby team members without the Curtain of Slumber shield gain a shield that absorbs 35% of the Curtain of Slumber's absorption amount. This shield lasts for 12 seconds and has 250% increased Cryo damage absorption. |
| **Comet Guidance** | When a Shooting Star from Nights of Formal Focus hits an enemy, Layla regenerates 1 Energy. Each Shooting Star can restore Energy once per hit. |

| **Starry Prophecy** | When Nights of Formal Focus begins shooting stars, all nearby party members receive the "Illumination" effect, increasing their Normal Attack and Charged Attack damage based on 5% of Layla’s Max HP. "Illumination" lasts for up to 3 seconds and is removed 0.05 seconds after a Normal or Charged Attack lands. |
| **Divine Guidance** | Increases the level of Dream of the Star-Stream Shaker by 3, up to a maximum of level 15. |
| **Celestial Beacon** | Increases the damage of Nights of Formal Focus’s Shooting Stars by 40% and Dream of the Star-Stream Shaker’s Starlight projectiles by 40%. Additionally, the interval between the generation of Shooting Stars in Nights of Formal Focus is reduced by 20%. |

**Recommended Builds**  

### Shield Support

| Artifact Set | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| 4-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith + Any | General pick for shield support |
| 4-Piece Noblesse Oblige | Alternative for buffing team damage |
| 2-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith + 2-Piece Vourukasha’s Glow | Alternative for maximizing shield strength |

**Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** HP% / Energy Recharge
- **Goblet:** HP%
- **Circlet:** HP%

**Recommended Substats**
- HP% > Flat HP >= Crit Rate (when using Favonius Sword) > Energy Recharge

**Recommended Weapons**
- **Best 5★:** Key of Khaj-Nisut (boosts HP and provides Elemental Mastery to teammates)
- **Best 4★:** Fleuve Cendre Ferryman (provides HP boost, ignoring its Energy Recharge effects)
- **Alternative 5★:** Freedom-Sworn (enhances party damage in certain setups)
- **Alternative 4★:** Favonius Sword / Sacrificial Sword (energy regeneration and shield uptime improvement)

### Off-Field DPS Build

| Artifact Set | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| 4-Piece Blizzard Strayer | Best for Frozen teams, boosting Crit Rate against frozen enemies |
| 2-Piece Blizzard Strayer + 2-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith | Alternative for teams that cannot maintain Cryo application on enemies |

**Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** HP%
- **Goblet:** Cryo DMG Bonus
- **Circlet:** Crit Rate / Crit DMG

**Recommended Substats**
- Crit Rate / Crit DMG > HP% > Flat HP > Energy Recharge

**Recommended Weapons**
- **Best 5★:** Primordial Jade Cutter (High Crit Rate and HP boost)
- **Best 3★:** Harbinger of Dawn (Free-to-play friendly with high Crit DMG boost when HP is above 90%)
- **Alternative 5★:** Key of Khaj-Nisut (Provides massive HP scaling and enhances Layla’s shield and damage)

---  

**Final Notes:**
Layla is an excellent Cryo support character who provides strong shields while dealing off-field damage through her skill and burst. Depending on the team composition, she can be optimized for maximum shield uptime or secondary Cryo DPS output in Frozen or Superconduct teams.
