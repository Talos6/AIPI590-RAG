Keqing is a versatile Electro character capable of dealing consistent elemental damage through fast attack combos. With the introduction of Aggravate reactions in Dendro-based teams, her Electro output has significantly improved. Depending on the build, Keqing can function as a standard Electro DPS, an Aggravate reaction DPS, or a Physical DPS. Her rapid attacks and teleportation ability make her a dynamic and flexible character for various team compositions.
