- **Sands:** Elemental Mastery (for reaction-based) or ATK%

- **Circlet:** Crit DMG / Crit Rate

**Recommended Skill Priority:** Normal Attack > Elemental Skill > Elemental Burst

**Best Team Compositions**  

1. **Yoimiya Vaporize Team** (Yoimiya + Yelan / Xingqiu + Bennett + Zhongli)  
   - Focuses on Pyro-infused Normal Attacks with Hydro application for Vaporize reactions. Zhongli provides shield and resistance reduction.  
   
2. **Yoimiya Overload Team** (Yoimiya + Beidou / Fischl + Thoma / Bennett)  
   - Uses Electro support for Overload reactions. Works well with Beidou’s coordinated Electro damage.  
   
3. **Yoimiya Pyro Hypercarry** (Yoimiya + Yun Jin + Bennett + Zhongli)  
   - Boosts Normal Attack damage using Yun Jin and Bennett’s buffs while Zhongli ensures stability.
