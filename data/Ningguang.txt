**Ningguang**

**Title**: Eclipsing Star  
**Full Name**: Ningguang  
**Nation**: Liyue  
**Race**: Human  
**Gender**: Female  
**Rarity**: 4-Star  
**Availability**: Permanent  
**Vision**: Geo  
**Weapon**: Catalyst  
**Constellation**: Opus Aequilibrium  
**Special Dish**: Qiankun Mora Meat  
**Release Date**: September 15, 2020 (Version 1.0)  
**Tags**: Resistance Buff, Damage Buff, Ore Detection, Geo Constructs  

**Introduction**: One of the "Liyue Qixing," known as the "Tianquan." Her wealth is beyond comparison across the entire continent.

---

### **Constellation**

| Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Piercing Fragments** | Normal Attacks deal AoE damage upon hitting an enemy. |
| **Shock Effect** | When Jade Screen is shattered, its cooldown is reset. This effect can occur once every 6 seconds. |
| **Majesty be the Array of Stars** | Increases the level of Starshatter by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **Exquisite be the Jade, Outshining All Beneath** | Jade Screen increases nearby characters' Elemental Resistance by 10%. |
| **Invincible be the Jade Screen** | Increases the level of Jade Screen by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **Grandeur be the Seven Stars** | When Starshatter is used, it generates 7 Star Jades for Ningguang. |

### **Recommended Builds**

#### **Geo DPS Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Song of Days Past* (best in slot)
  - 2-piece *Archaic Petra* + 2-piece *Gladiator's Finale*
  - 2-piece *Noblesse Oblige* + any 18% ATK set

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: ATK%
  - **Goblet**: Geo DMG Bonus
  - **Circlet**: CRIT DMG / CRIT Rate

- **Substats**:
  - CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG > ATK%

- **Skill Leveling Priority**:
  - **Main DPS**: Normal Attack > Elemental Burst > Elemental Skill
  - **Burst DPS**: Elemental Burst > Elemental Skill > Normal Attack

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *Lost Prayer to the Sacred Winds* (5★)
  - *Cashflow Supervision* (5★)
  - *Tulaytullah’s Remembrance* (5★)
  - *Kagura’s Verity* (5★)

- **Alternative Weapons**:
  - *The Widsith* (4★)
  - *Solar Pearl* (4★)
  - *Skyward Atlas* (5★)
  - *Memory of Dust* (5★)

#### **Support Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Instructor* (if team requires Elemental Mastery boost)
  - 4-piece *The Exile* (if team requires Energy Recharge support)

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: Energy Recharge
  - **Goblet**: Any piece with Energy Recharge substat
  - **Circlet**: Healing Bonus (if using Prototype Amber)

- **Substats**:
  - Energy Recharge

- **Skill Leveling Priority**:
  - Elemental Burst > Elemental Skill

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *Prototype Amber* (4★) (for healing support)
  - *Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers* (3★) (for ATK buffing)

#### **Beginner Build**

- **Recommended Artifact Sets**:
  - 4-piece *Martial Artist*
  - 2-piece *Berserker* / *Brave Heart* / *Gladiator’s Finale* + any strong piece

- **Main Stats**:
  - **Sands**: ATK%
  - **Goblet**: Geo DMG Bonus / ATK%
  - **Circlet**: CRIT Rate / ATK%

- **Substats**:
  - CRIT Rate, ATK%, CRIT DMG, Energy Recharge

- **Best Weapons**:
  - *The Widsith* (4★)
  - *Solar Pearl* (4★)
  - *Emerald Orb* (3★) (for early game)

---

Ningguang is an extremely powerful Geo character who excels in single-target damage and is very easy to build. With her unique playstyle revolving around her Jade Screen and Star Jades, she can be played effectively as a main DPS or a burst-support unit.
