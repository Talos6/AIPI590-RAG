## **Recommended Builds**

### **Artifact Sets**

#### **Best-in-Slot (BiS)**  
- **4-Piece Deepwood Memories** (for team Dendro RES reduction)  
- **4-Piece Noblesse Oblige** (if another character is using Deepwood)  
- **4-Piece Instructor** (for Elemental Mastery buff, but only 4★ max)  

#### **Alternative Options**  
- **4-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith** (for a **C2+ Support** build)  
- **4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate** (for higher Elemental Burst uptime)  
- **2-Piece Deepwood Memories + 2-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate**  
- **2-Piece Noblesse Oblige + any ATK% set**  

**Recommended Stats**
- **Sands:** Energy Recharge / ATK%  
- **Goblet:** Dendro DMG Bonus  
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG  
- **Substats Priority:** CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG > ATK% > Energy Recharge > Elemental Mastery  

---

### **Recommended Weapons**

#### **Best 5★ Weapons**  
- **Elegy for the End** – Best for team support (EM & ATK buff).
- **Polar Star** – High CRIT boost and consistent damage output.
- **Skyward Harp** – Increases CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG.

#### **Best 4★ Weapons**  
- **Fading Twilight** – Event weapon with high ER and extra DMG boost.  
- **Moonpiercer** – EM-focused weapon for Bloom/Hyperbloom teams.  
- **Alley Hunter** – ATK-based weapon for off-field DPS.  
- **Recurve Bow** – Budget HP-based alternative.  
- **Sacrificial Bow** – Good for **Dendro application consistency** with C2.  
- **Favonius Warbow** – Energy generation support weapon.

---

Collei is a **Dendro support and sub-DPS** who excels in applying **Dendro status** for reaction-based team compositions. Her **Energy Recharge scaling**, **team buffs**, and **off-field application** make her a great fit in **Dendro-based reaction teams** like **Aggravate, Bloom, and Quicken teams**. Her **C2+ allows better synergy** in quick-swap teams, and **Deepwood Memories** ensures she contributes effectively even when off-field.
