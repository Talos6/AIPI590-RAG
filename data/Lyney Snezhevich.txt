## **Constellations**

| Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Tricks of the Light** | Up to 2 Grinning Cat Hats can exist simultaneously. Additionally, when Lyney's Charged Attack - Prop Arrow hits an enemy, it summons 2 Grinning Cat Hats and accumulates an extra stack of "Prop Surplus." This effect can occur once every 15 seconds. |
| **Loquacious Lure** | While Lyney is on the field, he gains a "Focused Attention" effect every 2 seconds, increasing his CRIT DMG by 20%. This effect stacks up to 3 times and is removed when he leaves the field. |
| **Nimble Fingers** | Increases the level of Normal Attack: Card Force Translocation by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **Well-Rehearsed Mischief** | When Lyney’s Charged Attack hits an enemy affected by Pyro, their Pyro RES is reduced by 20% for 6 seconds. |
| **Stagecraft Precision** | Increases the level of The Wondrous Trick: Miracle Parade by 3, up to a maximum level of 15. |
| **A False Smile** | When Lyney fires a Prop Arrow, he also fires a Pyrotechnic Strike: Encore, dealing 80% of a Pyrotechnic Strike’s DMG as Charged Attack DMG. |

## **Recommended Builds**

### **Pyro DPS Build**

#### **Artifact Set Recommendations**
- **4-Piece Marechaussee Hunter**
- **4-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence (transitional option)**
- **4-Piece Crimson Witch of Flames (situationally viable)**
- **2-Piece Crimson Witch + 2-Piece Shimenawa’s Reminiscence**

#### **Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK%
- **Goblet:** Pyro DMG Bonus
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG

#### **Recommended Substats**
CRIT DMG > CRIT Rate > ATK% > Elemental Mastery > Energy Recharge

#### **Talent Priority**
Normal Attack > Elemental Skill = Elemental Burst

#### **Best Weapons**
- **5★ The First Great Magic**: Best-in-slot weapon, providing high CRIT DMG and synergy with Lyney's Pyro team compositions.
- **5★ Aqua Simulacra**: Offers the highest CRIT DMG bonus, making it a great alternative.
- **5★ Polar Star**: Provides CRIT Rate and overall DMG bonuses but requires stacking.
- **4★ The Viridescent Hunt**: Offers CRIT Rate and slight crowd control.

---

### **Physical DPS Build** *(Not Recommended)*

#### **Artifact Set Recommendations**
- **4-Piece Pale Flame**
- **4-Piece Bloodstained Chivalry**
- **2-Piece Pale Flame + 2-Piece Bloodstained Chivalry**

#### **Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK%
- **Goblet:** Physical DMG Bonus
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG

#### **Recommended Substats**
CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > ATK% > Physical DMG > Elemental Mastery

#### **Talent Priority**
Normal Attack only (Elemental Skill and Burst are not useful for this build)

#### **Best Weapons**
- **5★ Thundering Pulse**: Provides high ATK% and CRIT DMG.
- **4★ Rust**: Good for Normal Attack-based Physical DPS.
- **4★ Prototype Crescent**: Good for targeting enemy weak points.

## **Best Team Compositions**

### **Standard Pyro DPS Team** *(Best for Pure Pyro Lyney)*
- **Lyney** (Main DPS)
- **Bennett** (ATK buff, healing, and Pyro resonance)
- **Kazuha / Venti** (Crowd control and Pyro DMG boost via Viridescent Venerer)
- **Zhongli** (Shielding and resistance shred)

### **Pyro Reaction Team** *(For Reverse Vaporize or Melt Compositions)*
- **Lyney** (Main DPS)
- **Xingqiu / Yelan** (Hydro application for Vaporize)
- **Bennett** (ATK boost and healing)
- **Kazuha / Sucrose** (Elemental mastery boost and crowd control)

### **Physical DPS Team** *(Not Recommended but Possible)*
- **Lyney** (Physical DPS)
- **Rosaria** (Superconduct application and CRIT Rate boost)
- **Bennett** (Healing and ATK boost)
- **Mika** (Attack Speed and Physical DMG boost)

---

Lyney shines as a **Pyro DPS character**, leveraging **charged shots, crit scaling, and elemental buffs**. He pairs best with **Bennett, Kazuha, and Pyro resonance**, making him a **strong single-target specialist with flexible team compositions**.
