## Constellations

| Name | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Bound by Verification** | When "Still Frame: Comprehensive Confirmation" heals a character, they gain the "Verification" mark, restoring HP equal to 80% of Charlotte's ATK every 2 seconds for 6 seconds. |
| **Duty to the Truth** | When using "Framing: Frozen Composition," if "Mr. Whittington" hits 1/2/3 or more enemies, Charlotte’s ATK increases by 10%/20%/30% for 12 seconds. |
| **Independence First** | Increases the level of "Still Frame: Comprehensive Confirmation" by 3, up to a maximum of level 15. |
| **Responsibility to Supervise** | When "Still Frame: Comprehensive Confirmation" hits enemies marked with "Instant Snapshot" or "Focused Impression," damage dealt is increased by 10%, and Charlotte gains 2 Energy. This effect can trigger up to 5 times every 20 seconds. |
| **Principle of Conscience** | Increases the level of "Framing: Frozen Composition" by 3, up to a maximum of level 15. |
| **Relevance is Key** | When the current active character's Normal or Charged Attacks hit enemies marked with "Focused Impression" from "Framing: Frozen Composition," "Mr. Whittington" performs a coordinated attack, dealing Cryo DMG equal to 180% of Charlotte's ATK in an AoE. The active character also heals HP equal to 42% of Charlotte’s ATK. This effect can occur once every 6 seconds. The damage and healing from this effect are considered as Elemental Burst damage and healing. |

## Recommended Builds

### **Support Healer Build**

#### **Artifact Set Recommendations**
- 4-Piece Noblesse Oblige
- 4-Piece Song of Days Past
- 4-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith
- 4-Piece Maiden Beloved
- 2-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate + 2-Piece Maiden Beloved

#### **Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** Energy Recharge
- **Goblet:** ATK%
- **Circlet:** Healing Bonus

#### **Recommended Substats**
Energy Recharge, CRIT Rate (for Favonius Codex), ATK%

#### **Talent Priority**
Only level up Elemental Burst. Normal Attack and Elemental Skill do not need investment.

#### **Best Weapons**
- **4★ Prototype Amber**: Provides self-sustain and Energy Recharge, making it Charlotte’s best healing option.
- **4★ Oathsworn Eye**: High ATK, good Energy Recharge bonus, and overall strong synergy.

#### **Alternative Weapons**
- **4★ Favonius Codex**: Energy Recharge-focused, helps smooth team rotations.
- **4★ Wine and Song**: Provides Energy Recharge and ATK.

---

### **Cryo DPS Build**

#### **Artifact Set Recommendations**
- 4-Piece Hunter’s Dawn
- 4-Piece Blizzard Strayer
- 2-Piece Blizzard Strayer + 2-Piece Gladiator’s Finale

#### **Recommended Main Stats**
- **Sands:** ATK% or Energy Recharge
- **Goblet:** Cryo DMG Bonus or ATK%
- **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG

#### **Recommended Substats**
CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Energy Recharge

#### **Talent Priority**
Upgrade Elemental Burst first, followed by Elemental Skill.

#### **Best Weapons**
- **5★ The Widsith**: High burst damage potential.
- **5★ Kagura’s Verity**: High base ATK and strong DMG buffs.
- **5★ Skyward Atlas**: General ATK and Energy Recharge utility.

#### **Alternative Weapons**
- **4★ The Widsith**: Excellent for quick burst setups.
- **4★ Solar Pearl**: Balanced for Normal and Charged Attacks.
- **4★ Oathsworn Eye**: Energy Recharge and ATK buffs.

---

Charlotte can flexibly serve as a **healer** or **Cryo damage dealer** depending on the build. Her best performance comes from maximizing her **Elemental Burst** for healing, while for damage, she thrives with **Cryo DMG Bonus and CRIT stats**.
